Despite the availability of numerous government welfare schemes, many eligible citizens fail to benefit from them due to:

- Lack of awareness about existing and newly launched schemes  
- Complex eligibility criteria that are difficult to understand  
- Official documents written in technical or legal language  
- No mechanism to notify citizens when new schemes are introduced  

This information gap especially affects students, low-income groups, and people from rural or semi-urban areas.

## ğŸ’¡ Proposed Solution

Jan Sahayak acts as an intelligent informational assistant that bridges the gap between government welfare schemes and citizens.

The system also proactively informs users whenever a new government scheme matching their profile is launched, ensuring that no eligible citizen misses an opportunity.
